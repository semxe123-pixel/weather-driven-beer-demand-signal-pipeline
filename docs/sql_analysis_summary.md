## SQL-based Demand Signal Analysis

- beer_search_by_temp_bin.csv  
  Shows a clear increase in beer search interest as temperature rises,
  with a notable acceleration above 25°C.

- beer_search_by_weekday.csv  
  Indicates a strong weekend effect, with peak demand on Friday–Saturday.

- beer_search_temp_weekend_effect.csv  
  Demonstrates that weekend uplift exists across all temperature bins,
  and is most pronounced in the 20–30°C range.
